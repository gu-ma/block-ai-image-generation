# Practical Tools, Ethics, and Iterative Practice

## Current Tool Landscape (2026)

Commonly discussed image-generation tools and ecosystems include:

- **Flux**: strong quality and growing ecosystem adoption
- **Midjourney v7**: popular for fast aesthetic exploration
- **Stable Diffusion 3.5**: flexible open workflow options
- **ComfyUI**: node-based control for reproducible custom pipelines

Tool choice depends on your priorities: speed, control, customization, cost, and deployment context.

## Ethics Checklist for Creative Practice

### 1) Bias and Representation

- Training data can encode social/cultural bias.
- Always review outputs critically for stereotypes and exclusion.

### 2) Intellectual Property and Attribution

- Style and authorship boundaries remain contested.
- Keep transparent process notes (prompts, references, edits, model choice).

### 3) Environmental Cost

- Inference at scale has energy impact.
- Prefer efficient iteration habits: smaller batches first, upscale/select later.

## Hands-on Production Tips

1. **Iterative generation > one-shot generation**
   - Generate, evaluate, and refine in cycles.
2. **Use inpainting for targeted correction**
   - Fix local issues without regenerating the whole image.
3. **Track parameters**
   - Keep seed, steps, sampler, CFG, and prompt versions.
4. **Curate intentionally**
   - Select outputs based on project criteria, not novelty alone.
